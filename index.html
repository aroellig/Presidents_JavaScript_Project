Presidents Trivia

Test your knowledge of US presidents. Once you click begin you will be presented with an image of each US president. When you click on an image you will be presented with four facts. You will have one chance to choose the correct fact. You may use the score button at the top of the page to see how many correct facts you have chosen and once you have selected each president you will receive a final score
